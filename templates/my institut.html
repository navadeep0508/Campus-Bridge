About Mohan Babu University

Mohan Babu University (MBU), formerly known as Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Institutions, is a private university located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 1992, it was restructured into a university in January 2022.

History and Establishment

Mohan Babu University originated from the Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Trust (SVET), founded by Dr. M. Mohan Babu in 1992. The institution evolved from Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College, which was established in 1996, and expanded to include various schools offering diverse programs. In January 2022, the Andhra Pradesh State Legislative Assembly recognized the institution as a private university, renaming it Mohan Babu University.
